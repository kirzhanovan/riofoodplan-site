1. About Rio

Rio is a mobile app for people with food intolerances. It brings together information about 500+ foods and 7 common trigger chemicals: histamine, FODMAPs, salicylates, oxalates, sulfur, lactose, and gluten. Rio helps you spot patterns between your reactions and specific foods, based on your unique answers.

What Rio does:

  • Provides educational information about food chemicals
  • Lets you take a quiz to get a personalised tolerance profile
  • Generates a colour-coded food list based on your settings
  • Lets you adjust tolerance levels and mark foods based on your personal experience

What Rio does NOT do:

  • Diagnose medical conditions
  • Provide medical advice or treatment
  • Replace consultation with healthcare professionals
  • Guarantee any health outcomes
